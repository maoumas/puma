What is this?

SaraPuma is a bridge between Google NotebookLM and readability. It formats plain text into an "Apple Notes" style with color-coded side notes, quotes, and highlights.

Why Markdown?

Markdown is the universal language of text. It's safe, light, and compatible with everything. We just make it look beautiful and professional.
